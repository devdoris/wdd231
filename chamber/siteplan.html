Student Productivity & Study Planner – Site Plan

Site Name

MySuccessGuide

The name MySuccessGuide was selected because it represents a friendly and supportive guide tool that helps students succeed, stay organized, productive, and on track with their studies.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help students manage their time, stay productive, and organize their academic tasks. The site will provide:

  • Study tips and productivity guidance
  • A study planner for daily and weekly tasks
  • Helpful resources and tools for students

Scenarios

  • How can I organize my assignments and study schedule for the week?
  • What strategies can help me stay focused while studying?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Green (#2f4d30): Headings and highlights
  • Light Gray (#f0f0f0): Background areas
  • Accent Orange (#a0650d): Buttons and emphasis areas

Typography

  • Headings: Nunito, sans-serif
  • Body text: Nunito, sans-serif
